# 2025 Competition: For Authors

Help us build a challenging, high-quality question set for QANTA 2025. Accepted questions earn **$5 each**.

## Submission

Submit questions through the AI-assisted writing interface or via Google Sheets formatted per our template.

- **Interface**: [write.qanta.org](http://write.qanta.org)
- **Deadline**: June 7, 2025 (half-packet due June 1)

## Question Guidelines

### Difficulty
Questions should be at **"at least 3-dot"** difficulty — comparable to HSNCT/PACE NSC level. The first one or two lines should be unanswerable by baseline AI systems.

### Format
- **Length**: Approximately 450 characters (target range 400–700)
- **Type**: Tossups only (no bonuses)
- **Answers**: Must match Wikipedia page titles
- **Style**: Pyramid structure — hardest clues first, easier clues toward the end

### Themed Packets
QANTA 2025 questions are drawn from themed packets covering:
- Military history
- World literature
- Music
- Canadian history

### What Makes a Good Adversarial Question?

**Good questions** are adversarial if they:
- Paraphrase rather than quote standard clues
- Require multi-hop or logical reasoning
- Demand commonsense or expert-level knowledge
- Include mathematical elements
- Use cross-lingual references

**Avoid** questions that:
- Use vague location descriptors
- Contain false presuppositions
- Add subjective specificity that penalizes knowledge
- Use "swerve" structures that redirect expectations mid-question

A good question is one where a knowledgeable human immediately recognizes the correct answer, even if the AI does not.

## Scoring

Submitted questions are scored on four dimensions (25 points each):

| Criterion | Description |
|---|---|
| **Discriminability** | Distinguishes player skill levels via item response theory |
| **Difficulty** | Questions where humans outperform AI |
| **Diversity** | Varied entities, locations, named entities across the set |
| **Quality/Quantity** | Number of questions passing editorial review |

## Rules

- Submit under a single account at write.qanta.org
- Questions must be original ("clean") — not previously published
- No communication between question writers and computer competition teams
- Distribution follows PACE guidelines
