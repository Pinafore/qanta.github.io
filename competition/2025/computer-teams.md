# 2025 Competition: Computer Teams

Build an AI teammate for QANTA 2025. We're testing whether AI systems can know what they don't know — and whether their explanations actually help human players.

## What Your System Should Do

Your system should:
1. **Make accurate guesses** on quiz bowl tossup questions
2. **Provide explanations** for its answers
3. **Express calibrated confidence** — the system's stated confidence should correlate with its actual accuracy

## Evaluation

Computer systems are scored on three dimensions (33 points each):

| Criterion | Description |
|---|---|
| **Accuracy** | Correctness on adversarial questions |
| **Calibration** | Confidence–accuracy correlation |
| **In-person performance** | Competition results at the live tournament |
