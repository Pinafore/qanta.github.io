## How to Participate

There are three ways to join QANTA 2025:

### Build an AI Teammate
Design a system that makes accurate guesses with explanations and appropriate confidence levels. AI is great at answering questions — except when it isn't. Can your system know when it doesn't know? We test calibration, compare AI vs. human judgment, and rank systems on a leaderboard. Bonus rounds examine whether AI explanations actually help human players score higher.

→ [Computer Teams](/competition/2025/computer-teams/)

### Play as a Human
Bring your knowledge and strategy, then team up with an AI agent. See how human–AI collaboration stacks up against solo human or AI play.

→ [Human Teams](/competition/2025/human-teams/)

### Write Challenging Questions
Author quiz bowl questions and receive **$5 per accepted question**. Questions should be adversarial — easy for expert humans, hard for AI systems.

→ [For Authors](/competition/2025/authors/)

## Why Join?

AI is really good at answering questions — except when it isn't. Can systems know they don't know? QANTA 2025 explores:

- **Calibration**: Does AI confidence correlate with accuracy?
- **Human–AI teaming**: When does AI help humans, and when does it hurt?
- **Adversarial evaluation**: Which questions separate human from machine capability?
