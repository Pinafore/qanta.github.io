# 2025 Competition: Human Teams

Play as a human in QANTA 2025 and team up with an AI agent. Bring your knowledge and strategy — then see how human–AI collaboration stacks up against solo play.

## Prize

Best-performing human trivia team: **$200**

## Tournament Formats

**In-Person** — June 14, 2025, University of Maryland, College Park, MD

**Online** — June 21, 2025, open to participants worldwide

## Registration Deadline

June 10, 2025

## How It Works

1. Human players answer quiz bowl tossup questions
2. An AI teammate provides guesses and explanations alongside yours
3. You decide when to buzz and how to factor in AI input
4. Teams are ranked by overall score across the competition

## Who Should Play?

QANTA welcomes quiz bowl players of all experience levels — from seasoned tournament players to casual trivia enthusiasts. No prior quiz bowl experience is required.
